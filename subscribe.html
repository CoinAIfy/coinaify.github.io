Subscription Details

Everything you need to know about billing, trials, and cancellation.

🎁 Free Trial

New users receive a 2-day free trial with full access to all premium features. No payment is required to start. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your selected subscription will begin automatically.

🔄 Auto-Renewal

Monthly and Annual subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date. Your Apple ID account will be charged within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. Lifetime access is a one-time purchase with no renewal.

❌ How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time through iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → CoinAIfy. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You will retain access until that date.

♻️ Restore Purchases

If you have previously purchased a subscription or lifetime access and need to restore it on a new device, open CoinAIfy, go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

💰 Refunds

All purchases are processed by Apple and are subject to Apple's refund policy. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select the CoinAIfy purchase.

🔒 Privacy & Data

Images you scan are sent to our servers solely for AI identification and are not stored or shared. Your collection data is saved locally on your device. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

FAQ

Common Questions

Can I switch plans after subscribing?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the next renewal date.

Does the free trial require a credit card?

The free trial is managed entirely by Apple. You will need an Apple ID with a valid payment method on file, but you will not be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime before the trial ends to avoid charges.

What happens to my collection if I cancel?

Your collection data is stored locally on your device and is not deleted when you cancel. You can still view your saved items. Scanning new items and accessing premium features will require an active subscription.

Is the Lifetime plan really one payment forever?

Yes. The Lifetime plan is a single one-time purchase that grants permanent access to all current and future features of CoinAIfy. There are no recurring charges.

Can I use CoinAIfy on multiple devices?

Yes. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID. Sign in with the same Apple ID on any iPhone and use Restore Purchases in Settings to activate your subscription on that device.

How accurate are the AI valuations?

CoinAIfy provides estimated market value ranges based on AI analysis of the coin or banknote's visible condition and publicly available pricing data. Valuations are for reference only and should not be used as a substitute for a professional appraisal.
