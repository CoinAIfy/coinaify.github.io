2 Description of Service

CoinAIfy is an AI-powered iOS application that allows users to identify, grade, and estimate the market value of coins and banknotes by photographing them. The App provides:

  • Estimated grading and condition assessment
  • Estimated current market value ranges
  • Personal collection tracking and management
  • Nearby coin dealer and auction house finder
  • PMG/PCGS grade recognition for professionally graded notes
  • Serial number detection and classification for banknotes

The AI identification and valuation system is designed to provide helpful estimates based on visible condition and publicly available pricing data. However:
