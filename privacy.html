1 Introduction

CoinAIfy ("we," "us," or "our") is an iOS application that uses artificial intelligence to identify, grade, and value coins and banknotes from photographs. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use CoinAIfy, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By using CoinAIfy, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2 Data We Collect and How It Is Used

2.1 Coin and Banknote Images

When you scan a coin or banknote, the image is compressed on your device and transmitted to our server solely for AI identification. The image is passed to an AI vision model, which returns identification results (name, grade, estimated value, etc.). Images are never written to a database, file system, or any persistent storage. They exist only in server memory for the duration of the request — typically a few seconds — and are discarded immediately after the AI responds.

In plain terms: We cannot retrieve, review, or share your scan images. They are gone the moment the AI finishes processing them.

2.2 Scan Results and Collection Data

The AI identification result (coin name, country, year, grade, estimated market value, serial number, etc.) is returned to your device and saved locally using your device's on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

2.3 Location Data (Dealer Finder Only)

If you use the "Find Nearby Dealers" feature, the app requests your device's GPS coordinates. These coordinates are sent to our server, which forwards them to the Apple Maps Server API to search for nearby coin dealers and shops. Your location is not stored. It is used only for the duration of the search query and discarded immediately after results are returned.

2.4 Subscription and Purchase Data (RevenueCat)

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management platform. We receive only your subscription status (active/inactive) and the plan type (monthly, annual, or lifetime). We never receive your Apple ID, payment card details, or billing address. RevenueCat's own privacy policy governs their data practices: revenuecat.com/privacy.

2.5 Anonymous Install Attribution (SKAdNetwork)

Apple's SKAdNetwork (SKAN) framework sends privacy-preserving, anonymous install-attribution postbacks to our server when the app is installed via an advertisement. These postbacks contain no personal information — they are aggregated, delayed, and designed by Apple to be privacy-safe. The data we store from these postbacks is:

  • A unique transaction ID assigned by Apple (not linked to any individual)
  • The ad network and campaign that drove the install
  • An aggregate conversion value (e.g., "trial started") — not individual behaviour
  • Whether the attribution "won" (Apple's attribution logic)
  • The timestamp the postback was received

This data is used solely to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns at an aggregate level. It cannot be used to identify any individual user.

3 Third-Party Services

CoinAIfy uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • AI Vision Model (Gemini 2.0 Flash via Manus Forge): Receives your scan images for identification. Images are not retained by the AI provider after processing.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status and in-app purchases. See revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit: Processes all payments. See apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Apple Maps Server API: Used for the dealer finder feature. Receives GPS coordinates only during a search query. See apple.com/legal/privacy.

CoinAIfy does not integrate any advertising SDKs, social media SDKs, analytics platforms, or crash reporting tools.

4 Data Retention

On-device data (your collection, scan results, subscription state) is retained until you delete the app or clear its data in iOS Settings. We have no ability to access or delete this data on your behalf.

Anonymous SKAN attribution data is retained on our servers for up to 36 months for aggregate advertising analytics, then deleted.

Scan images and location data are never retained — they are discarded immediately after processing.

5 Children's Privacy

CoinAIfy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because CoinAIfy does not collect personal information from any user, there is no special risk to children's privacy. .

6 Your Rights

Because CoinAIfy does not collect personal information tied to your identity, most traditional data subject rights (access, correction, deletion) apply only to the data stored locally on your device, which you control directly.

  • Access your collection data: It is stored on your device and visible within the app at all times.
  • Delete your collection data: Delete the app or clear its data in iOS Settings → CoinAIfy → Reset.
  • Manage subscription data: Contact RevenueCat or manage through iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Opt out of SKAN attribution: You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

If you have questions about your data or wish to exercise any rights, contact us at coinaify@gmail.com.

7 Security

All data transmitted between the app and our server is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Scan images are processed in server memory and never written to disk. We do not store personal data, which means there is no personal data to be breached.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. However, because we do not retain images, location data, or personal identifiers, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal information is minimal.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will provide notice within the app. Continued use of CoinAIfy after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
