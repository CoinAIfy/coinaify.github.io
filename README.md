5. AI Processing and Image Data
When you photograph or upload an image of a coin or banknote, that image is processed by an AI model to perform identification, grading, and valuation. Please be aware of the following:

•	Images submitted for identification may be transmitted to a remote AI processing service to generate results.
•	Images are used solely for the purpose of identification and are not stored on our servers beyond the time required to generate a result.
•	We do not use your coin images to train AI models without your explicit consent.
•	Identification results, grades, and valuations are returned to your device and stored locally. They are not associated with your identity on our servers.


6. Data Storage and Security
All collection data, scan history, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device using your device's secure storage mechanisms (AsyncStorage / device keychain). This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly use the Share or Save to Photos features.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


7. Data Retention
Because CoinAIfy stores data locally on your device, you are in full control of your data retention. You may delete your scan history, collection, and favorites at any time within the App. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data from your device.
